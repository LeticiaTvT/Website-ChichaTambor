Chicha Fuerte Production

This is an alcoholic beverage that is more persistent in the indigenous communities and nearby provinces in Panama based on corn born or new corn, also this chicha consists mainly in the fermentation of corn. It is a very traditional chicha, since it comes from a long time ago and persists in our country.



STEP 1 : SOWING AND HARVESTING

Corn planting and harvesting process.

STEP 2: MOVING AND CLEANING

The corn is placed in containers and moved to clean it.

STEP 3: CORN GERMINATION

The corn is washed and put to germinate.

STEP 4: PREPARING THE CHICHA

It is washed again, and the corn is milled, finally, it is cooked...

STEP 5: FERMENTATION

The chicha is stored in barrels and left to ferment for approximately one week.

STEP 6: PACKAGING

The chicha units are filled and packaged for distribution.
